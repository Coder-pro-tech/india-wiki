History of India

All you need to know about the history of this nation to understand it better:

Mauryan Empire

Founded by Chandragupta Maurya, it is one of the largest empires of India.

Under Ashoka, the empire grew significantly. It covered almost all of India and some parts of Pakistan.

Chola Dynasty

It was founded by Vijayalaya Chola. It ruled over the south of India and some parts of southern Asia.

The empire was at its peak when Rajendra I ruled over it. They also built many famous temples.
